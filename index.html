USE YOUR VOICE FOR A HEALTHY PREGNANCY

Becoming a mom can be exciting. The journey during and after pregnancy is an adventure that requries a supportive team of friends, family, and health care providers.

This time can also be when medical complications could happen, and sometimes even death for a mother and her baby if her health concenrs are not treated. In 2014, as many as 50,000 women in the U.S. experienced severe, unexpected health problems relaated to pregnancy that may have long-term health consequences* - but pregnancy-related deaths are preventable.
More than 700 women die each year in the U.S. from complications related to pregnancy or delivery.
